The iCracked Lifetime Warranty

iCracked offers a comprehensive lifetime warranty on all our repairs? Would you trust your most cherished smart device to anyone who didn't? Yup, we didn't think so either.

Scope of Warranty Coverage

  • Screen/LCD and any other repaired or replaced parts that malfunction, or do not work as intended or designed.
  • Warranty is limited to the parts and/or service(s) that were paid for. If only parts were purchased, warranty is limited to the replacement of the parts. If parts and repair service were purchased, warranty extends to cover the labor cost of part replacement and any other repairs specifically resulting from the initial repair, except in the situations outlined below.

Warranty Does Not Protect Against

After iCracked repairs the device, the warranty does not protect against:

  • Subsequent mishandling that causes the frame to bend, twist, or crack
  • Water damage
  • Subsequent accidental or purposeful drops
  • Tampering with internal hardware
  • Damage resulting from attempted customer repairs
  • Software issues unrelated to the repair
  • Jailbroken devices
  • New damages unrelated to the original repair
  • Any loss of data occurring as a result of the repair - customers are advised to back up all data prior to repair attempt.
  • Battery replacements.  Batteries are considered a consumable product and iCracked does not offer a lifetime warranty on battery replacements.

Our warranty also does not cover the outcome of a repair if certain pre-repair conditions exist, including:

  • Existence of known manufacturing and/or performance issues related to the device separate from the repair, as noted prior to the repair
  • Existence of damage to the frame of device, as noted prior to the repair
  • Water damage
  • Jailbroken devices
  • Tampering with internal hardware: under certain conditions, internal damage may make a repair impossible. Your iTech or repair specialist will be able to explain in further detail upon diagnosing your specific device. If in doubt, we recommend that you do not attempt to repair on your own, as any damage may affect the reparability of your device.
  • a NON-Working, damaged or severed home button/Bio-Metric scanner

The warranty is valid only for the specific device repaired and the original customer; it is not transferable across devices or if the device is sold or given to another individual.

If a customer device is irreparably damaged as a direct result of a repair attempted by an iTech and payment has been rendered, the customer is entitled to a fully functional refurbished device of equal or greater value of the damaged device based on its model and condition as received and with the original repair issue resolved, or the monetary equivalent of fair market value of the device in that condition as determined by iCracked HQ. If payment was not rendered for service, the repair quote shall be deducted from the value, or a refurbished device of the same damaged condition as received shall be offered. In order to receive a replacement device, the customer's damaged device must be rendered to iCracked.

  • In the case that the repair is performed by an iTech, the iTech is responsible for the replacement and should be contacted directly. iTech Customers are also welcome to contact iCracked HQ to notify us of a repair issue, however, liability shall rest with the iTech who performed the service.
